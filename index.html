With IMI, you can choose both! Jai is a certified professional yoga therapist and a life coach. Monte is a professional mental health therapist and Tai Chi Instructor who works extensively with meditation and mindfulness-based techniques.

According to Trauma-Informed Care, trauma "results from exposure to an incident or series of events that are emotionally disturbing or life-threatening with lasting adverse effects on the individual's functioning and mental, physical, social, emotional, and/or spiritual well-being."

IMI works with mindfulness practices to elevate subconscious thoughts to help overcome some of the adverse effects that trauma has on our lives. We all experience trauma in different ways. Working to live mindfully can provide peace of mind and personal confidence.

Yes! IMI works with veterans and the unique challenges they face. Mindfulness and meditation-based activities are used extensively through Department of Veterans Affairs programs.

"Veterans suffering from PTSD find that mindfulness-based stress reduction reduces the severity of PTSD symptoms."
- Results from a clinical trial conducted by Dr. Melissa A. Polusny, Minneapolis VA Medical Center

Mindfulness is the practice of being present and fully engaged with whatever we're doing at the moment, free from distraction or judgment. Our approach combines traditional mindfulness techniques with modern therapeutic practices.

Benefits include reduced stress and anxiety, improved emotional regulation, better sleep, enhanced focus, and greater overall well-being. We help you develop practical mindfulness skills that you can use in daily life.
