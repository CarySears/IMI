Meet Jai & Monte McPheron

As a husband and wife team, we started IMI because of a strong passion for helping people overcome mental health trauma and create a better world. Our combined 45+ years of experience brings together diverse therapeutic approaches.

Jai is a certified professional yoga therapist and life coach, specializing in holistic wellness approaches.

Monte is a professional mental health therapist and Tai Chi instructor who works extensively with meditation and mindfulness-based techniques.

Together, we offer a unique blend of traditional therapy and innovative mindfulness practices to support your journey toward mental wellness.
